Our Advisor's Message

It gives me immense pride to witness the growth of LIMUN into a dynamic platform that empowers young minds to engage with the most pressing global challenges of our time. As the academic advisor for LIMUN’25, I wholeheartedly commend the passion, commitment, and vision of the organizing team, who continue to transform this simulation into a hub of diplomacy, critical thinking, and inclusive dialogue. LIMUN is more than a conference; it is a learning experience that nurtures future diplomats, leaders, and changemakers. It challenges delegates not only to understand international affairs but to develop empathy, negotiate ethically, and advocate with purpose. In an era where collaboration is more crucial than ever, such platforms help students rise above differences and discover common ground. To all participants of LIMUN’25 — may this journey inspire you to think boldly, speak thoughtfully, and lead responsibly. Let this be a milestone in your personal and intellectual growth. 
I extend my best wishes for a successful and transformative LIMUN’25.

Dr. Rabia Faridi
Incharge DCUAF
At LIMUN, we empower youth through diplomacy, dialogue, and debate. We simulate the United Nations to nurture critical thinking and leadership. Our conferences bring together students from diverse backgrounds and regions. We provide a platform for meaningful discussions on global challenges. Through workshops, training sessions, and committees, we ensure holistic learning. LIMUN is more than a conference : it’s a movement for informed global citizenship.
